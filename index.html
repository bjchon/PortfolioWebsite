About Me

Greetings and welcome to my website! I am a curious adventurer navigating through life with an unwavering passion for technology, data, full stack web development, art, and photography. My journey has been a mosaic of experiences, from visiting every state in America (minus Hawaii and Alaska) to hiking the tallest peaks of Yosemite, shaping my fascination with human civilization and mother nature. I revel in the art of learning, constantly seeking to broaden my horizons and unearth hidden gems in both the ordinary and the extraordinary.

In my downtime, I find solace in watching my Lakers play, working out at the gym, spending time with my dogs, friends, and family, embracing moments of serenity and joy. I cherish authenticity, empathy, and the transformative power of connections, aiming to leave a positive footprint in the world while embracing the wonders of this exhilarating ride we call life.
